Transmission 5-speed manual synchromesh Don’t go on the size, Renault Kwid though small, offers a really good value for money composition. The cute little hatch comes with some top of the life features like a 7″ infotainment system. That is not all, this system supports Android Auto and Apple CarPlay. Even the built quality is good for its price.

Talking about the powerhouse, Renault Kwid comes with 2 petrol engine options, an 800cc and a 1000cc 3 cylinder engines. The smaller 800CC engine puts out 54ps of peak power and 72Nm of peak torque. But if you want more power, 1000CC produces 68ps of max power and 91Nm of peak torque. Both of the engines are mated to a 5-speed manual transmission with the later one getting an AMT option.
